Stoicism
Stoicism is a philosophy that teaches the pursuit of inner peace through wisdom, self-discipline, and focusing only on what we can control. By embracing virtue and accepting life's challenges calmly, Stoics aim to live a meaningful and fulfilled life.
Pillars of Stoic Thought
"You have power over your mind, not outside events."
"Luck is what happens when preparation meets opportunity."
"Live in harmony with nature."
"True strength comes from within."
“It never ceases to amaze me: we all love ourselves more than other people, but care more about their opinion than our own.”
― Marcus Aurelius, Meditations
